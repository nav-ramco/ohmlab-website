Smart Home Use Cases

Real-world applications of edge AI vision and audio in residential and connected home environments.

Local Voice Control

Process voice commands entirely on-device — no wake word sent to the cloud, no latency, no subscription fees. Full privacy for every household.

Presence & Occupancy Detection

Detect room occupancy and activity patterns using on-device vision, enabling smart lighting, heating and security responses without any cloud connectivity.

On-Device Security Cameras
