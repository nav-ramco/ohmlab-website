Healthcare Use Cases

Real-world applications of edge AI vision and audio in medical and clinical environments.

Vital Signs Monitoring

Continuous on-device monitoring of heart rate, respiratory patterns and movement anomalies using vision AI — no wearable contact required.

Medical Imaging Analysis

Run lightweight inference models at the point of care for wound assessment, skin condition detection and anomaly flagging — all locally on the Neuro N6.

Fall Detection

Detect patient falls in real time using on-board vision models, triggering immediate alerts without any cloud round-trip or latency.

Audio Clinical Monitoring

Classify respiratory sounds, coughs and distress calls using on-device audio AI — supporting early detection in wards, care homes and home health settings.

Surgical Assistance

Provide real-time visual guidance and instrument tracking during procedures using edge vision models with sub-millisecond inference times.

Privacy-First Design

All AI inference runs locally — no patient images or audio ever transmitted to the cloud, meeting GDPR and clinical data governance requirements.
