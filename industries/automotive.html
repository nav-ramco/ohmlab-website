Automotive Use Cases

On-device AI for safer, smarter vehicles — no connectivity required.

👤

Driver Monitoring

Detect drowsiness, distraction and gaze direction in real time using on-board vision AI — works in all lighting conditions.

🚶

Pedestrian Detection

Identify and track pedestrians, cyclists and vulnerable road users at the edge with sub-10ms latency for ADAS systems.

Gesture Control

Enable in-cabin gesture recognition for infotainment, HVAC and other controls — processed locally, instantly.

Monitor cabin audio for emergency voice commands, baby cries or abnormal sounds with on-device audio classification.

Parking & Surround Vision

Process multiple camera feeds at the edge for surround-view parking assistance and obstacle detection.
