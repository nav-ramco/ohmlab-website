Agriculture Use Cases

Real-world applications of edge AI vision and audio in farming and food production.

🌿

Crop Health Monitoring

Detect disease, nutrient deficiency and water stress in real time using visual AI models trained on crop imagery — no internet connection required.

🐛

Pest & Weed Detection

Identify specific pest species and invasive weeds at the edge, triggering targeted interventions and reducing pesticide usage by up to 80%.

🤖

Autonomous Field Robots

Give ground robots real-time visual awareness for navigation, obstacle avoidance and task execution — no latency, no cloud dependency.

📈

Yield Estimation

Count fruit, measure canopy density and estimate yield pre-harvest using on-board vision models running on the STM32N6 NPU.

🔄

Irrigation Intelligence

Combine visual soil condition analysis with sensor data to make precise, localised irrigation decisions without cloud connectivity.

🔊

Audio Monitoring

Detect machinery faults, animal distress or environmental events with on-device audio classification — all processed locally on the Neuro N6.
