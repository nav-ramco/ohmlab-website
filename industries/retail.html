Retail Use Cases

Real-world applications of edge AI vision and audio across physical retail and commerce environments.

Loss Prevention

Detect shoplifting behaviour and shelf theft in real time using on-device vision AI — no cloud uploads, instant local alerts, GDPR-compliant by design.

Footfall & Heatmap Analytics

Count customers, map movement patterns and identify high-dwell zones entirely on-device, giving store operators actionable layout insights without any data leaving the premises.

Shelf Intelligence

Automatically detect empty shelves, misplaced products and planogram compliance violations using on-board vision models running on the STM32N6 NPU.

Queue Management

Monitor checkout queue lengths and customer wait times in real time, enabling dynamic staffing decisions — all processed locally at the edge.

Smart Inventory

Track stock levels visually using edge cameras, triggering replenishment alerts and reducing out-of-stock incidents without manual scanning or cloud connectivity.

Audio Event Detection

Classify unusual sounds — alarms, breaking glass, raised voices — using on-device audio AI to enhance in-store security and incident response.
