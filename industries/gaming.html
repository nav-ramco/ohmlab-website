Gaming Use Cases

Real-world applications of edge AI vision and audio in gaming hardware and interactive entertainment.

Eye Tracking

Enable gaze-based interaction for VR headsets, gaming monitors and accessibility controllers using on-device vision processing with zero cloud latency.

Gesture Recognition

Detect hand poses, swipes and complex gestures in real time for motion-controlled gaming — no wearable sensors required, just a camera and the Neuro N6.

Handheld Console AI

Power on-device object detection and scene understanding in portable gaming devices running on battery with ultra-low power consumption.

Voice Command Processing

Run speech recognition and voice command models locally for in-game voice control with instant response times and complete privacy.

Player Emotion Detection

Analyse facial expressions in real time to adapt gameplay difficulty, trigger events or create responsive NPCs — all processed locally on the STM32N6 NPU.

AR/Mixed Reality

Enable real-time object recognition and spatial mapping for augmented reality gaming experiences running entirely on-device without cloud processing.
